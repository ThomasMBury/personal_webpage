I am an applied mathematician at McGill University doing research in the labs of Gil Bub and Leon Glass.


My current research concerns cardiac arrhythmias - abnormalities in the beat rhythm of a heart, some of which can lead to cardiac arrest. For more information on this or my other research interests, please visit my 'Research' page, or interact with simulations on the 'Animations' page.
