I am an applied mathematician interested in mechanistic modelling and statistical analysis of biological systems. I am currently posted at the Department of Physiology, McGill University, co-advised by Dr Gil Bub and Dr Leon Glass.


My current research concerns cardiac arrhythmias - abnormalities in the beat rhythm of a heart, some of which can lead to cardiac arrest. For more information on this or my other research interests, please visit my research page.
